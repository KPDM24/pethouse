About Us

We are a group of committed citizens, determined to make a change in the way stray dogs are managed in Oradea.We believe that Oradea lacks a vision for the management of stray dogs.

Our shelter has been a beacon of hope for abandoned and mistreated animals for over two decades. We provide a temporary home, medical care, and love to pets in need while seeking a forever home for each of them. Our mission goes beyond shelter walls – we advocate for animal rights and work towards a future where every pet is safe and loved.

Together, we can make a difference. Join us in this vital cause.

Our Goals

Pet Welfare Collaboration

Association agreement between the Municipality of Oradea and ADI Zona Metropolitană Oradea for free microchipping, identification, registration and sterilization campaigns.

Community Awareness Initiatives

Awareness campaigns for the inhabitants of Oradea and the metropolitan area.

Open Access Shelter Surveillance

Video surveillance for the public shelter for stray dogs in the Municipality of Oradea that can be freely accessed via the Internet by any citizen

Shelter Expansion Project

Increase the capacity of the Grivei shelter to 300 places.
